Hidden Pandemic

By: Lauren Stipp, Same Fewkes, Sercan Sengun

There are multiple pandemics ravaging the globe. As a politician, will you put public health and safety first or focus on your own interests? Just kidding! You are a politician; of course, you will serve your own interests.

Hidden Pandemic was created during the spring 2020 class CTK 359 - Games and Soceity. With this game I helped come up with the base idea. For the final version I created the visual look of the box and cards.
