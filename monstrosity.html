Monstrosity

By: Lys Bump, Flynn Geraghty, Lauren Stipp, Khadijah Yarrow

Raise your monster to defeat your opponents! Allow your monster to grow by strategizing against your opponents through gaining or stealing elemental magic and unlocking powerful abilities. The last one standing with their monster wins!

Monstrosity was created during the spring 2020 class CTK 380.5 - Introduction to Game Design (Now CTK 351 - Game Design I). I helped come up with the rules of the game in order to make the game enjoyable for others to play.
