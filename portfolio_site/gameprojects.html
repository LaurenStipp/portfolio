Monstrosity

A collaborative game that I helped create in my Game Design class. The goal of the game is to raise a baby monster to be an adult and be the last one to survive. Players trade in resources to attack or to grow into an adult dragon.

Odif

Another collaborative game that I helped program in my Game Development class. This game is unfinished due to time constraints and what the group wanted to do with the game. The goal of the game is to defeat the monster dog that the town is "worshipping".

The Relationship Between Gaming and Personalities

A video essay I helped develop. The video essay links the impact personalities can have on games. Depending on how a person interacts in the real world may be reflected in the way they play games.

Mythical Kitchen

Mythical Kitchen is a game designed and developed by me for my Game Design class. The goal of the game is have the most points. To earn points players combine ingrediants together. Some ingrediants DO NOT go with each other and make the player lose points. The game is played for 3 rounds. The player with the most points at the end wins the tile of Best Mythical Chef.

Buyer's Market

A collborative game created for my Game Design class. The game is about realtors selling garbage apartments to a buyer. The goal of the game is for the realtors to sell the most garbage apartments. For each round the winning realtor becomes the new buyer.

Hidden Pandemic

A game in collabration with a classmate is about player trying to get the vaccine from the doctor. For this game I focused on making the look of the cards while my classmate worked on the rulebook.
